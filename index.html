Our Story

Family recipes, brought to your table

"Thank you for supporting a local dream — we can't wait to welcome you in and share our flavors with the community."

Ann's Noodle is a family-run kitchen bringing together the bright, bold flavors of Thailand, Laos, and Vietnam — from fragrant beef bone pho and spicy papaya salad to crispy pork belly and sweet boba tea.

Every bowl is built from scratch: slow-simmered broths, hand-prepared sauces, and ingredients our family has trusted for generations. Come hungry, leave full.
